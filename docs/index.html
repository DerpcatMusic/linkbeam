Three things, done well.

Built for one job: getting a fan from your ad to the play button, and keeping the tracking honest.

Loads before they scroll away

Fan pages are plain HTML from Cloudflare’s edge, so the buttons are tappable almost instantly.

Tracks from your side

Page views and stream clicks can be sent from your own domain with server-set Meta identifiers.

All of it is yours

Your Cloudflare account, your database, your domain, your Meta pixel. No per-click rent.

Set it up on Cloudflare.

  • Create D1, KV, R2, and queue resources.
  • Paste the generated IDs into wrangler.jsonc.
  • Add Meta and optional Spotify credentials as Worker secrets.
  • Run migrations, deploy, and publish your first link.
